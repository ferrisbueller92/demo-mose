Evidence, not hype

Doses you can point to a study for.

Every active is here because the research backs it — and we deliberately dose at the well-tolerated lower end. Enough to do the job, gentle enough for daily use.

Evidence-led

Only ingredients with real human trials — RCTs and meta-analyses, not hype.

Conservative by design

Modest daily doses, often below the maximal clinical amount, so more people tolerate them well.

Nothing to hide

Exact amounts on the label, each mapped to the study behind it. No proprietary blends.
